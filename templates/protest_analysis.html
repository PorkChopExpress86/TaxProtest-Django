ℹ️ How to Use This Analysis

Equity Protest (Texas §41.43)

Texas law requires "equal and uniform" appraisal. If your property is appraised at a higher ratio than comparable properties of the same kind and character, the appraisal district must prove otherwise by preponderance of the evidence. The comparable table below is direct evidence for this argument.

Exchange evidence with the ARB at least 5 business days before your hearing date.

Market Value Protest

A separate argument: your appraised value exceeds what a willing buyer would pay. This requires recent sale prices (MLS data). This tool uses HCAD assessed values, which are a proxy — not sale prices. Supplement with MLS comps from a real estate professional for a market value argument.

Submitting Your Evidence

  • Print 3 copies of this report to bring to your ARB hearing.
  • Upload evidence to HCAD's online protest portal at least 5 days before your hearing.
  • Include photos of property condition issues if relevant.
  • The protest deadline is typically May 15 or 30 days after your notice of appraised value, whichever is later.
